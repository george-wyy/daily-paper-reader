## 摘要
返回抑制（IOR）是指相对于新位置，对先前检查过位置呈现的刺激的反应时间（RTs）变慢。然而，IOR 发生的具体处理阶段及其在不同反应模式下的性质仍存在争议。通过使用单一噪声累加器模型重新分析来自目标-目标 IOR 范式的 RT 数据，我们测试了 IOR 是发生在处理的感觉或注意阶段，还是发生在决策和动作选择的后期阶段。我们考虑了两种条件下的 IOR：手动反应和眼跳反应。试次内高斯噪声参数最能解释手动和眼跳 IOR，这表明在两种模式下，IOR 可能源于重复位置证据积累的更大波动。这些结果支持了目标-目标 IOR 可能主要涉及注意水平机制的假设。意义陈述：我们对短时间内在同一位置呈现的刺激反应较慢（“返回抑制”），这种偏向被认为能促进高效的视觉探索。通过对两项先前研究中的手动和眼动反应时间进行证据积累建模，我们发现与返回抑制相关的关键变化是证据积累中更大的试次内变异性（噪声），而不是更高的决策阈值。了解受影响的处理阶段有助于将行为效应与支持注意和定向的大脑网络联系起来。

## Abstract
Inhibition of return (IOR) refers to the slowing of response times (RTs) for stimuli presented at previously inspected locations relative to novel locations. However, the exact processing stage(s) at which IOR occurs, and its nature across different response modalities, remain debated. By reanalyzing RT data from a target-target IOR paradigm with a single noisy accumulator model, we tested whether IOR could occur at sensory or attentional stages of processing, or at later stages of decision and action selection. We considered IOR under two conditions: manual and saccadic responses. The within-trial Gaussian noise parameter best explained both manual and saccadic IOR, suggesting that in both modalities, IOR may result from a more fluctuating accumulation of evidence for repeated locations. These results support the hypothesis that target-target IOR may primarily involve attentional-level mechanisms.

Significance statementWe respond more slowly to a stimulus that is presented within a short interval in the same location ("inhibition of return"), a bias thought to promote efficient visual exploration. Using evidence-accumulation modeling of manual and eye-movement reaction times from two previous studies, we found that the key change linked to inhibition of return is greater within-trial variability (noise) in evidence accumulation, not a higher decision threshold. Understanding which processing stage is affected can help connect behavioral effects to the brain networks that support attention and orienting.
