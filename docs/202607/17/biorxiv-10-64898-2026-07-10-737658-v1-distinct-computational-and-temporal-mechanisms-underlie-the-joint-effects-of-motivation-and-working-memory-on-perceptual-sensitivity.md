## 摘要
人类感知持续受到内部认知状态的影响，然而动机和工作记忆如何共同影响感知敏感性仍所知甚少。在此，我们结合行为实验、计算建模和瞳孔测量法，以确定动机是通过放大工作记忆驱动的促进效应来增强感知，还是两者施加独立影响。参与者在一个系统操纵动机和工作记忆状态的近阈限视觉空间辨别任务中进行测试。行为上，动机和工作记忆均独立改善了感知表现，当两者同时存在时改善最大。一个贝叶斯广义线性心理测量模型揭示，这些改善最好由对有效感知敏感性的独立叠加贡献来解释，而非动机对工作记忆的放大。与此计算框架一致，瞳孔动态追踪了试次间有效感知敏感性的波动，同时揭示了感知决策过程中工作记忆和动机在时间上可分离的影响。总之，我们的发现表明动机和工作记忆的联合效应源自不同的计算和时间机制，为理解人类感知的自上而下调控提供了一个统一框架。

## Abstract
Human perception is continuously shaped by internal cognitive states, yet how motivation and working memory jointly influence perceptual sensitivity remains poorly understood. Here, we combined behavioural experiments, computational modelling, and pupillometry to determine whether motivation enhances perception by amplifying working memory (WM)-driven facilitation or whether both exert independent influences. Participants performed a near-threshold visuospatial discrimination task under systematically manipulated motivational and WM states. Behaviourally, both motivation and WM independently improved perceptual performance, producing the greatest enhancement when both were present. A Bayesian generalized linear psychometric model revealed that these improvements were best explained by independent additive contributions to effective perceptual sensitivity, rather than motivational amplification of WM. Consistent with this computational framework, pupil dynamics tracked trial-by-trial fluctuations in effective perceptual sensitivity while revealing temporally dissociable influences of WM and motivation during perceptual decision making. Together, our findings demonstrate that the joint effects of motivation and working memory arise from distinct computational and temporal mechanisms, providing a unified framework for understanding top-down regulation of human perception.
